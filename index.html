Pioneer Forwarders Ltd is a medium sized, seasoned Supply and Logistics company founded in 2009

Pioneer Forwarders is Kenyan based company, owned by it's managers and managed by it's owners, with no foreign ownership or outsider investment that can adversely influence corporate policies and direction.

Pioneer Forwarders operates in a dynamic environment with continuous growth in the import, export and trans-shipment business. We aspire to be a major player in this thriving industry not only within our base in Kenya, but also within the region as a whole
